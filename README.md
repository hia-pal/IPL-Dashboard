# IPL Team & Player Statistics Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive sports analytics dashboard for the Indian Premier League (IPL), designed to analyze player performance, auction investments, team performance across venues, and season trends. The dashboard enables users to evaluate whether player auction prices align with on-field performance and how teams perform under different playing conditions.

By combining player-level and team-level metrics, the dashboard provides actionable insights into squad valuation, venue strengths, and team consistency throughout the season.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze the relationship between player auction prices and performance.
* Compare team performance across different stadiums and venues.
* Examine auction spending patterns among franchises.
* Evaluate team scoring trends over time.
* Identify high-value and undervalued players.

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Price vs Performance Analysis

A scatter plot comparing player auction price with performance score.

**Insights**

* Identifies players delivering strong performance despite lower auction prices.
* Highlights expensive players who may be underperforming.
* Allows comparison across teams using color-coded markers.
* Bubble size represents player strike rate.

---

### 2. Venue-Wise Team Performance Heatmap

Displays average team performance across major IPL venues.

**Insights**

* Reveals venue-specific strengths and weaknesses.
* Helps identify teams with strong home-ground advantages.
* Enables comparison of performance consistency across stadiums.

---

### 3. Team Auction Price Distribution

Box plots showing the distribution of player auction prices for each franchise.

**Insights**

* Compares squad-building strategies.
* Highlights franchises investing heavily in star players.
* Identifies teams with balanced spending structures.

---

### 4. Auction Spending Breakdown

A pie chart illustrating the proportion of total auction expenditure by each team.

**Insights**

* Visualizes team investment allocation.
* Compares franchise spending behavior.
* Identifies teams with the largest auction commitments.

---

### 5. Team Performance Over Time

A time-series visualization tracking team runs scored throughout the season.

**Insights**

* Measures consistency in batting performance.
* Detects periods of strong and weak form.
* Supports trend analysis across teams.

---

## Key Findings

* Auction price does not always translate into higher player performance.
* Several lower-priced players deliver performance comparable to premium auction picks.
* Team performance varies significantly across venues, highlighting the importance of playing conditions.
* Some franchises exhibit greater consistency throughout the season, while others show higher volatility.
* Spending patterns differ substantially across teams, reflecting different squad construction strategies.
